Blob Sol — Solana Experiment

This is an interactive experiment on the Solana blockchain. A creative trial exploring how art, music, and decentralized technology can intersect — merging the world of machine learning with the spirit of Web3.

Play four opera voices in real time — no singing skills required. A machine learning model was trained on 16 hours of professional opera singing to understand what opera sounds like. Drag the blobs to create your own music.

Take the blobs on tour to cities around the world — London, New York, Mexico City, Seoul, Cape Town, and Paris — and hear them sing classic songs from each location.
